Digital Posture

Digital posture refers to the overall readiness, maturity, and resilience of an organization’s digital capabilities. Think of it as the organization’s “stance” in the digital economy—how well it can leverage technology to achieve strategic goals, adapt to disruption, and remain competitive.

Why should I care?

Just like physical posture, training is pointless if done incorrectly. It requires proper movements supported by the right equipment, a personalized program with balanced nutrition, and correct standing. These are the same three dimensions essential for approaching digital posture.

How to assess

The best way to assess your Digital Posture is through a structured maturity model assessment that gives you a holistic view of where your organization stands. A maturity model provides a framework to evaluate how advanced your organization is across critical dimensions—strategy, processes, technologies, governance, and culture.

The 3 Dimensions

By structuring assessments into these three dimensions, organizations can balance people readiness, technical resilience, and external digital reputation—a holistic view of their digital posture.
