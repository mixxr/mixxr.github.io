Activities & Assessments

  • IT Infrastructure Audit → Review cloud adoption, system integration, scalability, and redundancy.
  • Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity Testing → Conduct tabletop exercises, failover simulations, and recovery time objective (RTO) assessments.
  • Cybersecurity Posture Evaluation → Perform vulnerability scans, penetration testing, and compliance checks (ISO 27001, NIST, GDPR).
  • Data Management & Governance Review → Assess data quality, accessibility, and protection policies.
  • Operational Resilience Benchmarking → Compare incident response readiness and downtime tolerance against industry standards.

Executive Summary

These assessments ensure the organization’s digital presence is reliable, secure, and customer‑centric. Infrastructure audits and resilience benchmarking reduce downtime and performance issues that harm user experience and conversion; disaster recovery and continuity testing ensure rapid restoration of customer‑facing services to meet SLAs; cybersecurity evaluations and data governance protect customer information and regulatory compliance; and data improvements enable better personalization and support analytics. Together they deliver prioritized, evidence‑based recommendations to lower operational cost, increase customer trust, and accelerate engagement and retention across digital channels.
